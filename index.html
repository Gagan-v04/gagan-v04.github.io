About Me

               
                   

                        Hello! I'm Gagan, a passionate engineer with a strong foundation in Mechanical Engineering from Ramaiah Institute of Technology. My curiosity isn't just about how things are built, but how we can make them think, adapt, and interact with the world.                    

                   

                        This drive led me to the intersection of physical systems and artificial intelligence. My professional experience has been focused on building and simulating autonomous systems, from high-performance search-and-rescue UAVs to sophisticated industrial cobots.                    

                   

                        I'm currently a Robotics & ML Intern at Sapien Robotics, where I'm leading an integration project for Schneider Electric, leveraging tools like NVIDIA Isaac Sim, ROS 2, and YOLO to bring autonomous solutions to life.
